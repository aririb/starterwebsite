My Summer 2023 Photo Diary

All of the memories I made this summer!

This summer was a mix of professional development and unforgettable experiences, and I think it was my best summer yet. First, I finished the last two months of my first six-month co-op, which allowed me to apply the computer science knowledge I've gained in college to real-world projects. My role was in product management which I really grew to enjoy.

In addition to my work, I made the most of my free time by exploring visiting New York City and Chicago. Attending two music festivals was definitely a highlight since I've never attended one before. "Head in the Clouds" was my favorite since it featured some of my favorite artists as well as highlighed Asian musicians.

After the end of my co-op, I served as a peer leader for SoarCS. It was very rewarding to mentor fellow computer science majors, particularly freshmen. Throughout all of this, I spent time exploring Boston and spending quality time with friends, making this summer an unforgettable chapter of my college journey.
